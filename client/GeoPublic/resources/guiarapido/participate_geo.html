1. First time here? You must register yourself to participate. This will take just a few minutes. Or you can use a social network to login.
    Use the button to register.
  2. Authenticate to participate.
    Use the button to login.
  3. Check other participations. You can comment existent participations.
  4. WWhen you feel comfortable, you can add new participations. Click on the New participation bar to show the participation form.
    1. Choose a title for the participation.
    2. Write a clear description.
    3. Use the button to report the location. (required).
    4. Add documents of images to the discussion.
